# Aegis Protocol - Seed Round

## One-Liner
The trust infrastructure for the $52B agent economy. NeMo Guardrails safety + USDC settlement on Solana.

## Problem
AI agents are making millions of autonomous transactions daily - but there's no way to verify the skills they're invoking are safe, reliable, or honest. Payment rails exist (x402, Stripe MPP). Trust rails don't.

## Solution
Aegis Protocol: a marketplace where every AI skill invocation is safety-checked by NVIDIA NeMo Guardrails, quality-scored by a validator network, settled in USDC on Solana, and backed by slashable bonds.

## Business Model
6-way fee split on every invocation:
- Creator: 60% | Validators: 15% | Stakers: 12% | Treasury: 8% | Insurance: 3% | Burned: 2%

## Competitive Advantage
1. Only protocol with NeMo Guardrails integration (NVIDIA partnership)
2. Solana-native (65% of agentic payments settle on Solana)
3. USDC settlement (no token risk for creators)
4. 5-dimensional trust scoring (not just uptime)
5. Composable royalty system (skills pay upstream dependencies)
