# Aegis Protocol. Brand Style Guide

## Colors
- Background: #09090b (bg-0), #18181b (bg-1), #27272a (bg-2)
- Accent: #10B981 (emerald-500)
- Accent hover: #059669 (emerald-600)
- Accent muted: rgba(16, 185, 129, 0.15)
- Text primary: #fafafa (white)
- Text secondary: #a1a1aa (zinc-400)
- Text tertiary: #71717a (zinc-500)
- Border: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.06)

## Typography
- Font family: Aeonik, -apple-system, system-ui, sans-serif
- Headings: Aeonik, light weight, tight tracking (-0.02em)
- Body: 13-14px regular weight
- Labels: 10-11px uppercase, tracked (0.08em)
- Mono: JetBrains Mono, SF Mono, Fira Code

## Components
- Cards: bg-1 background, 1px border at 6% white, 6px border-radius, no shadows
- Buttons: sharp corners, emerald accent or white
- Status dots: 6px, emerald for active, zinc-500 for inactive
- Hover states: background shift only, no color change
- Scrollbars: 4px wide, white at 10% opacity

## Logo Usage
- Full wordmark: /assets/fullvectorwhite.svg
- Icon only: /assets/vectorwhite.svg
- Minimum size: 24px height for icon, 80px for wordmark
- Always white on dark backgrounds
- Never stretch, rotate, or add effects

## Voice
- Swiss minimal: clean, precise, confident
- No emoji in formal contexts
- Data-driven: always cite numbers
- Expert tone: we understand the market deeply
- No hedging: "we built" not "we hope to build"
